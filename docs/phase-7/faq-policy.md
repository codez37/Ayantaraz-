# FAQ Policy

## Management
FAQ items are content type `faq` with lifecycle:
draft → review → published → archived

## Content Rules
- Question: 10-200 characters, clear and specific
- Answer: 50-2000 characters, accurate and helpful
- No speculative or unverifiable claims
- Tax/legal FAQs must cite relevant law/article where applicable
- "برای اطلاعات دقیق با کارشناس مشورت کنید" disclaimer on tax items

## Sources
FAQs can be:
- Created directly by admin/content-manager
- Extracted from approved articles
- Suggested by support team

## Chatbot Integration
All published FAQs with `visibility=public` are automatically available to the chatbot knowledge base. The chatbot search query includes both `knowledge_base` and `faq` content types.
